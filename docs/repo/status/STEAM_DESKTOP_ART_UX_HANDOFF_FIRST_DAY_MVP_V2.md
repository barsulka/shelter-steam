# STEAM_DESKTOP — Art UX Handoff — First Day MVP v2

Date: 2026-07-06

Role: Art Director / Visual Design / UX

Status: PASS as prototype readability handoff. NOT ACCEPTED as production art.

## Summary

The First Day MVP v2 capture pack is accepted as a readable prototype proof. The sequence is understandable: first route, dachshund and bicycle association, payload return, storage/kitchen/packing/van flow, delivery confirmation, postcard moment, dog attention, slippers reward, and calm next-day hint.

The pack is not accepted as final art, final UI, final animation language, final dog identity, final emotional pacing, or final compact semantic readability.

## Main Art UX conclusion

R-23 improved readability, but meaning still depends too much on cards, labels, badges, arrows, and marker text. This is acceptable for a prototype proof. The next pass should move the main meaning into world objects, object states, dog poses, and simple animations.

## Accepted at prototype level

- Horizontal strip composition is more stable than v1.
- Main landmarks are visible: route area, storage, kitchen, packing/board zone, van/dog cluster.
- Dog Card and route/order card separation helps distinguish shared delivery success from the dachshund personal reward.
- The next-day hint tone is calm and non-pressuring.
- State proof confirms delivery completion, postcard, dog notice, reward, and reward equipped events.

## Not accepted as final

- Production art.
- Final dog silhouette and motion language.
- Final world-only readability without UI cards.
- Final compact readability at 96px.
- Final calm pacing and emotional pause.

## Key cues

### Dachshund as first driver

Prototype pass. Needs stronger final language through silhouette, bicycle proximity/use, route-prep pose, return posture, and a small driver gesture or prop.

### Labrador as calm helper

Partial prototype pass. Needs grounded walk, careful carry pose, steady packing pose, and calm idle near work zones.

### Postcard moment

Prototype pass. Needs a physical board moment: postcard appears on board, dogs notice it, pause or turn toward it, and the highlight fades into a persistent world state.

### Comfortable slippers reward

Improved but not final. Needs visible slippers or paw-level accent on dachshund plus a short equip or comfort beat. Dog Card may mirror the result but must not be the only source of meaning.

### Next-day hint

Tone accepted. Should become a physical note/sticker on the board or near the packing table, with soft appearance and no timer or pressure.

## Temporary scaffolding to keep

- Route/order card.
- Dog Card.
- Postcard card.
- Driver/helper badges.
- Payload, storage, van-ready markers.
- Postcard marker.
- Slippers marker.
- Next-day text marker.
- Hide UI / Show UI review control.
- 216 / 144 / 96 compact preview outputs.

These are useful as debug/review scaffolding, not final visual language.

## Replace with object/state/animation language

- Role badges -> silhouettes, gait, poses, props.
- Labels/arrows -> visible carried resources and physical handoff states.
- Van-ready marker -> open/loaded van state.
- Postcard card dominance -> physical board/postcard attention beat.
- Slippers label -> visible reward on dachshund.
- Floating next-day hint -> physical note/sticker.

## Low-speed capture requirement

Next capture pack should include:

- 1x low-speed visible loop.
- Optional 2x review capture.
- Hidden UI screenshots after key beats.
- Compact previews 216 / 144 / 96.
- State proof through Workbench / connector.
- Video frames around postcard and slippers moments.

100x capture remains useful for state proof but does not replace visual/player-feel review.
