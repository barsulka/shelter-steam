## Purpose

First transport for off-screen resource trips. It physically leaves and returns with cargo.

## Must

- Read as small bicycle with basket.
- Support Dachshund departure/return staging.
- Show visible return payload or have clear cargo attachment point.
- Stay small enough for strip readability.

## Must Not

- Become a car, truck or large delivery system.
- Become a shop/garage.
- Hide returned cargo.
