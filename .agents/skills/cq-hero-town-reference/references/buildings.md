# CQ Hero Town Buildings

## Observation Protocol

For each building:

- Screenshot before click.
- Click visible building body, not blank space above it.
- Screenshot after click.
- Record whether the camera centers on the building.
- Record panel title, level, visible stats, buttons, and close behavior.
- Avoid purchase/external/account actions without explicit confirmation.
